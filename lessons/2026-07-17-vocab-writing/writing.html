✍️ Paragraph Writing Guide

Topic: A Favorite Destination / Vacation

📝 1. Brainstorming with Wh- Questions

Choose 3–5 questions to answer. Your answers become the supporting sentences of your paragraph.

📍 Where — Location
  • Where is this destination exactly located?
  • Where do you usually stay (hotel, resort, homestay, tent)?
👥 Who — People
  • Who do you usually go there with (family, best friends, alone)?
  • What are the local people like (friendly, helpful, welcoming)?
🏖️ What — Activities & Sights
  • What are the most famous landmarks or tourist attractions there?
  • What do you usually do there (swimming, hiking, taking photos)?
  • What is special about the local cuisine?
  • What is the weather like?
⏰ When — Time
  • When did you last visit this place?
  • When is the best time of the year to visit?
🚗 How — Experience
  • How do you travel there (by plane, by train, driving)?
  • How do you feel when you spend time there?
❤️ Why — Reasons
  • Why is this your absolute favorite destination?
  • Why would you recommend this place to others?

Bộ đề thi thử năm 2024 — Phần Writing Part 1 & 2

📐 Part 2 — Opinion Essay (80–100 từ)

"Should children take part in community activities or not? Why?"
Câu 1 — Mở bài: Nêu quan điểm

Introduction: State your opinion

Giới thiệu chủ đề + đưa ra quan điểm rõ ràng.

I strongly believe that children should take part in community activities.
Câu 2 — Thân bài: Lập luận + Ví dụ

Body: Reasons + Examples

Đưa ra 2–3 lý do kèm ví dụ cụ thể.

Firstly, these activities help children develop important skills such as teamwork, leadership, and communication. For instance, when they join a neighborhood cleanup, they learn how to work with others and solve problems together.

Secondly, taking part in community activities teaches children about social responsibility. When they help elderly people or clean up public parks, they realize that contributing to society is meaningful and rewarding.
Câu 3 — Kết bài: Nhấn mạnh quan điểm

Conclusion: Reinforce your opinion

Tóm lại và nhấn mạnh lại quan điểm.

In conclusion, I believe that children should regularly participate in community activities because these experiences shape them into responsible, confident, and empathetic individuals.

💡 Bài mẫu hoàn chỉnh

I strongly believe that children should take part in community activities. Firstly, these activities help children develop important skills such as teamwork, leadership, and communication. For instance, when they join a neighborhood cleanup, they learn how to work with others and solve problems together. Secondly, taking part in community activities teaches children about social responsibility. When they help elderly people or clean up public parks, they realize that contributing to society is both meaningful and rewarding. In conclusion, I believe that children should regularly participate in community activities because these experiences shape them into responsible, confident, and empathetic individuals.
~97 từ

📐 Part 1 — Viết lại câu (Câu 41–45)

41. I can't get better grades because I don't study hard enough.
Gợi ý: Câu điều kiện loại 2 — If I studied...
42. The students spent 3 hours cleaning the school yard before the ceremony.
Gợi ý: It took + time + to-infinitive
43. "Does the bus go to the city center?" Isable asked the driver.
Gợi ý: Reported speech — if + past tense
44. He can find the solutions to maths problems faster than me.
Gợi ý: So sánh bằng — as...as
45. Both teenagers and adults use TikTok widely.
Gợi ý: Câu bị động

💡 Checklist — Tự kiểm tra bài Writing

✔ Đủ số từ: 80–100 từ
✔ Quan điểm rõ ràng: Đồng ý hoặc Không đồng ý
✔ Ít nhất 2 lý do + ví dụ cụ thể
✔ Từ vựng đa dạng, không lặp từ
✔ Ngữ pháp đúng (thì, chủ-vị, mạo từ)
✔ Dùng cụm nối câu: Firstly, Secondly, In conclusion...
✘ Không viết dưới 70 từ
✘ Không lạc đề, phải trả lời đúng câu hỏi
✘ Không chỉ liệt kê — phải nói rõ "có" hay "không"
